What is the DIG Framework?

The DIG framework is a structured approach to analysis that breaks down content into three levels:

  • Data: Raw facts, figures, and observations from the content
  • Information: Patterns, insights, and meaning derived from the data
  • Guidance: Actionable recommendations and next steps based on the information
